Answers before you call

Common Questions

Do you provide emergency plumbing service?

Yes. Priority scheduling is available for active leaks, sewer backups, water heater failure, and urgent drain problems.

Can I get pricing before work starts?

Yes. A technician confirms the issue, explains the options, and reviews pricing before repair work begins.

What areas do you serve?

Federal Way, Auburn, Kent, Des Moines, Tacoma, Fife, Milton, Edgewood, and nearby South Sound communities.

Do you repair water heaters?

Yes. We service common electric and gas water heater issues and can advise when replacement is the better value.
